Trojan Horse

Trojan Horse is a type of malware that disguises itself as a legimtimate tyoe of software as peer the user need but benath it lies the bunch of malware or viruses that might disrupt the computer system. Trojan Horse is one of the most widely used malware in today's time. Due to wide use of Trojan Horse majority of people are facing computer related problems every year. Trojan Horse are designed to damage user's files, redirect traffic, monitor the user's activity, steal sensitive data or set up backdoor access points to the system.Trojans Horse may delete, block, modify, leak or copy user's data, which can then be sold back to te user for ransom or on the dark web.

In order for people to be save from Trojan Horse people should follow responsible online behaviour. People should strictly avoid the use of pirated websites or apps. Avoiding random clicking on various links should be practised. People should also use strong password and use password manager so that their password can remain secured all the time. Using of two way verification should be implemented which will give attackers hard time exploiting other people accounts. Lastly, people should practice the use of getting good anti-virus rather than downloading some random anti-virus from internet. With this minor steps people can prevent their computers from getting Trojan attack to large extent.

But what a person can do if their computer has already been breached through malware attacks? At the very least people must download anti-virus in their computer cause it will be key aspect in detecting malware attacks and call a specialist in the matter. If a person hasn't downloaded an anti-virus and believes that their computer has been attacked by malware then they should immediately close of their computer and call a computer specialist so that they can use precautive measure and make computer free from malware.
